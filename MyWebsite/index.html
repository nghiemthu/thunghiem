See my feature games

Owl wheel

Owl wheel is about a little owl that will evolve to be a king. Your mission is to control the wheel in order to destroy the bullets. The wheel has 4 colors that prevent 4-colored bullet.

Game feature:
— Owl animation and evolvement
— Wheel rotation
— Color detection
— Leaderboard and achievement

Eye stick

Eye stick is also an endless game like owl wheel. Your mission now is to help those eyes get passed the green pipes. Those eyes moving by rotating their stick, help them stop their stick in the right time.

Game feature:
— Eye animation
— Stick rotation
— Control changing.
— Leaderboard and achievement

Speedo

Speedo is a casual card game. The video game is based on a real card game that we(me and my friends) call speedo. Introduced by my japanese friend, this game is our favorite card game.

Game feature:
— Card movement
— Potion collection
— Card shop and Instruction
— Leaderboard and achievement
— Time and potion countdown

Spini

Spini is my first published game. The game looks easy but actually pretty hard. It does not take a good review from my friends but someone likes it :) so well it is still my first publised game.

Game feature:
— Animals changing.
— Jumping and collecting stars
— Color changing.
— Leaderboard and achievement
